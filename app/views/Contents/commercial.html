  Semiconductors and Synthesizable Cores

  • The Western Design Center - The source for purchasing the latest in the 65xx family of Microprocessors, Microcontrollers, Microperipherals, Development Tools, Starter Kits and IP. WDC has been a pioneer in the licensing of microprocessor intellectual property (IP) cores for FPGA and ASIC development. We are the original source of the patented W65C02. This has evolved into a product line of hard and soft cores, development systems, consulting services and standard product ICs from stock.
  • Evatronix - An IP core developer and supplier that sells the C6502 products, synthesizable IP cores that are compatible with the 6502.
  • Invector Embedded Technologies - Produces embedded controller boards including the IET6502SBC, a small SBC with a 65C02, 65C22, four-port UART, 128K RAM, ethernet controller, and connections for a graphical LCD display.
  • Sitronix Technology Co. - A fabless LCD driver IC and SoC IC (microcontroller) provider based in Taiwan. Their product line includes microcontrollers based on the 65C02, such as the ST20P64.
  • Sunplus Technology - A microcontroller IC design house which makes a number of specialized 6502-based chips for consumer products, some of which include speech and LCD interfaces.
  • Realtek Semiconductor Corp. - Also produces a line of 6502-based consumer ICs. Their large website has complete information about their products and datasheets in PDF format.
  • Micronas GmbH - Microcontrollers used in consumer applications like televisions, matrix displays, projectors, VCRs, VTRs, satellite receivers, etc. as well as in automotive applications like dashboards, body and chassis control units based on the 65C02/65C816 core with high grade of integrated functionality.
  • Sierra Circuit Design, Inc. - Offers several synthesizable cores, including a 65C02 and a superset called the 65CX8 with eleven additional instructions such as MPY (multiply).

  Development Tools

  • The Western Design Center - WDC offers Hardware Development Tools which includes a developer board for each of our microprocessors and microcontrollers. The developer board connects to the PC via the parallel port. WDC also offers Software Development Tools, which includes a C Compiler, Assembler, Linker, and Debugger, that supports all of WDC's microprocessors and microcontrollers.
  • Avocet Systems, Inc. - Makers of embedded systems development software. Their 6502 products includes Avocet's AVSYS Real Time Operating System (RTOS) and AVA assembler, as well as the popular 2500AD assembler (ADA), 'C' compiler (ADC), and simulator (ADS). Avocet also makes ADC-65, which includes the ADC-65 C Compiler, Macro Assembler, Simulator, and IDE. It's a fully-integrated Windows package, allowing you to develop and debug in the same environment. A demo is available of ADC-65 is available here.
  • Crossware Products - Cross-development software tools for DOS and Windows 95/NT. Their product lineup includes two 65C02 cross-assemblers.
  • DataRescue IDA Pro - A powerful multi-processor interactive disassembler available for several different operating systems.
  • IAR Systems - The IAR Embedded Workbench offers an Integrated Development Environment for the Western Design Center 65C02/65C816 microprocessors which includes a 'C' compiler.
  • I-Pal Communications - Produces RICE65, an In-Circuit-Emulator (ICE) for the 65C02 which runs up to 8 MHz.
  • Micetek [archive.org] - A Taiwanese company called Micetek made a product called "EasyPack 6502F", an In-Circuit-Emulator (ICE) for the WDC W65C02 which runs up to 14 MHz.
