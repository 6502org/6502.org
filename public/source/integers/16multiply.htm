16-bit Multiple

It multiplies the 16 bits word in (0-1) by the 16 bits word in (2-3) and stores the result in (4-5). This method is better when we have to multiply by small values (below 16). The other way is faster for values above 16. 

;32 bit multiply with 64 bit product

MULTIPLY:  ldy #16
           lda #0
           sta 4
           sta 5
LOOP1:     asl 4
           rol 5
           asl 0
           rol 1
           bcc SKIP1
           clc
           lda 4
           adc 2
           sta 4
           lda 5
           adc 3
           sta 5
SKIP1:     dey
           bne loop1
